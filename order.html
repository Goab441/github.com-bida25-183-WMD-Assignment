Welcome to the order page

Here you can place your order for my crocs. Please follow the instructions below to place your order.

To place an order for my crocs, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to the home page, click the about page button to view available crocs options.There are more options here in this page.
  2. Choose your preferred crocs style and size.
  3. Go to the contact page to see my email address.
  4. Send an email to my email address including these details: name, phone number, type of crocs, size and color.
  5. Proceed to checkout and fill in your details.
  6. Review your order and submit.
